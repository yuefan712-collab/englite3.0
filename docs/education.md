# EngLite and Education (SDG4)

EngLite directly addresses barriers to quality education by simplifying
the decoding layer of written English.

## Educational Impact

EngLite helps learners by:
- Reducing spelling memorization
- Making pronunciation predictable
- Supporting learners with dyslexia
- Lowering entry barriers for non-native speakers

## Alignment with UN SDG 4

EngLite supports Sustainable Development Goal 4:
"Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all."

By reducing orthographic complexity, EngLite improves:
- Early literacy outcomes
- Accessibility in digital education
- Equity in global English education

## Use Cases

- Classroom reading support tools
- Dyslexia-friendly learning materials
- AI-assisted education platforms
- Low-resource learning environments
