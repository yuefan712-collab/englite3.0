# EngLite FAQ

## Is EngLite a new language?
No. EngLite is a logical spelling system for English, not a new language.

## Will EngLite replace English?
No. EngLite is designed to coexist with traditional English.

## Who is EngLite for?
- Learners and students
- People with dyslexia
- Non-native English speakers
- Educators and researchers
- AI and language technologists

## Is EngLite open source?
Yes. EngLite is released under the MIT License.

## Why does EngLite matter?
Because language should not be a barrier to learning.
